Places & Work

A life spent moving.

I grew up in Italy and studied in Bologna, where I got my BSc in Statistical Sciences and my MSc in Data Science. During my MSc I spent a year at Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution (WHOI) in Massachusetts — my first real dive into ocean acoustics and bioacoustics research.

After graduating I worked as a Research Associate and Data Scientist at the German Oceanographic Museum (DMM) in Stralsund, Germany, and started volunteering as a Data Scientist for the NGO Centile Patagonia. I'm now based in Marseille, doing my PhD at CNRS–LMA on underwater acoustics and seismology as part of a Marie Curie Doctoral Network.
